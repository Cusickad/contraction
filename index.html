This is the sort of contract you would use when you're making a large or important purchase or selling off something large or important, like land or equipment. However, you would also use this contract if you're involved with a series of small purchases, such as a supply agreement. In other words, if you own something and want someone else to own it, or you want to buy something somebody else has, this is the contract for you.

This is the contract when you're borrowing property or loaning property. It includes rental agreements for things like cars, lease agreements for housing, and use agreements for event spaces. If you own the property and still want to own it when the other person is done with it, or if you don't own the property but just want to use it, this is the contract you're looking for.

This is the contract you need if you're hiring someone or being hired, for short term or for long term. It's for both contractors and employees. If you're paying someone to do work, or being paid primarily for your services, this is the contract you want.

This is for any other contract. It will start out general, but will give more options for customization as you work through it. If none of the other contracts apply to your situation, start here.
